Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Watson ("the app") is developed by Schisler Family Studios. This policy explains what data the app collects — which, in short, is none beyond what stays on your own device.

Data Collection

Watson does not collect, transmit, store on any server, or share any personal or sensitive user data. The app has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no network connection to any Schisler Family Studios server or any third party.

Local Storage

Watson stores game information — such as player names, hand sizes, suggestion history, and past game results — directly on your device using standard browser/app storage. This information never leaves your device, is never transmitted anywhere, and is only accessible to you. Uninstalling the app or clearing its storage permanently deletes this information.

Third-Party Services

Watson does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Watson does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children, because it does not collect information from anyone at all.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same URL with a revised "Last updated" date.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to the developer, Schisler Family Studios, via the contact information listed on the Google Play Store listing for Watson.

Trademark Notice

Watson is an unofficial companion app for the board game Clue and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Hasbro.
